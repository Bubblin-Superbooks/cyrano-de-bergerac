CYRANO:
Who on God's earth is that?

A CADET (whispering):
It is a man
Who joined to-day.

CYRANO (making a step toward Christian):
To-day?

CARBON (in a low voice):
Yes. . .his name is
The Baron de Neuvil. . .

CYRANO (checking himself):
Good! It is well. . .
(He turns pale, flushes, makes as if to fall on Christian):
