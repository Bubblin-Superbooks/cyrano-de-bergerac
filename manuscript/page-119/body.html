CYRANO:
Come all—the Doctor, Isabel, Leander,
Come, for you shall add, in a motley swarm,
The farce Italian to this Spanish drama!

ALL THE WOMEN (dancing for joy):
Bravo!—a mantle, quick!—my hood!

JODELET:
Come on!
