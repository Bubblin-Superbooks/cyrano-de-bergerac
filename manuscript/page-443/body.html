ROXANE:
Wig-maker. . .

LE BRET:
Teacher of the lute. . .

ROXANE:
What will he be to-day, by chance?

RAGUENEAU (entering hurriedly):
Ah! Madame! (He sees Le Bret):
Ah! you here, Sir!

ROXANE (smiling):
Tell all your miseries
To him; I will return anon.

RAGUENEAU:
But, Madame. . .

(Roxane goes out with the Duke. Ragueneau goes toward Le Bret.)
