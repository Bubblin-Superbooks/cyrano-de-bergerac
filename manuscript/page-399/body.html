ROXANE:
Ah! how you err! 'Tis now that I love best—love well! 'Tis that
Which is thy true self, see!—that I adore! Were your brilliance dimmed. . .

CHRISTIAN:
Hush!

ROXANE:
I should love still! Ay, if your beauty should to-day depart. . .

CHRISTIAN:
Say not so!

ROXANE:
Ay, I say it!

CHRISTIAN:
Ugly? How?

ROXANE:
Ugly! I swear I'd love you still!
