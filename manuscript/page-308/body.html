DE GUICHE:
Come, make an end! I want. . .

CYRANO:
Oh-ho! You are sly!

DE GUICHE:
Sir!

CYRANO:
You would worm all out of me!—the way
The moon is made, and if men breathe and live
In its rotund cucurbita?

DE GUICHE (angrily):
No, no! I want. . .

CYRANO:
Ha, ha!—to know how I got up?
Hark, it was by a method all my own.
