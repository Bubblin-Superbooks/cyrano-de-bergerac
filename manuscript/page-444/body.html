Scene 5.

Le Bret, Ragueneau.

RAGUENEAU:
Since you are here, 'tis best she should not know! I was going to your friend just now—was but
A few steps from the house, when I saw him
Go out. I hurried to him. Saw him turn
The corner. . .suddenly, from out a window
Where he was passing—was it chance?. . .may be! A lackey let fall a large piece of wood.

LE BRET:
Cowards! O Cyrano!
