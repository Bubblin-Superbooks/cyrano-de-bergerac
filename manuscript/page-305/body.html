In Venice? ha!—or Rome?

DE GUICHE (trying to pass):
A lady waits. .

CYRANO (quite reassured):
Oh-ho! I am in Paris!

DE GUICHE (smiling in spite of himself):
The fool is comical!

CYRANO:
You laugh?

DE GUICHE:
I laugh,
But would get by!

CYRANO (beaming with joy):
I have shot back to Paris! (Quite at ease, laughing, dusting himself, bowing):
