ROXANE:
Who's that?

CHRISTIAN:
Christian!

ROXANE (disdainfully):
Oh! you?

CHRISTIAN:
I would speak with you.

CYRANO (under the balcony—to Christian):
Good. Speak soft and low.

ROXANE:
No, you speak stupidly!

CHRISTIAN:
Oh, pity me!

ROXANE:
No! you love me no more!
