BORE:
No, on the contrary!. . .

CYRANO:
Why then that air
Disparaging?—perchance you think it large?

THE BORE (stammering):
No, small, quite small—minute!

CYRANO:
Minute! What now?
Accuse me of a thing ridiculous! Small—my nose?

THE BORE:
Heaven help me!

CYRANO:
'Tis enormous! Old Flathead, empty-headed meddler, know
That I am proud possessing such appendice.
