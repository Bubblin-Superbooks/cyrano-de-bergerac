CYRANO:
Yes!—I exaggerate!

LE BRET (triumphantly):
Ah!

CYRANO:
But for principle—example too,—
I think 'tis well thus to exaggerate.

LE BRET:
Oh! lay aside that pride of musketeer,
Fortune and glory wait you!. . .
