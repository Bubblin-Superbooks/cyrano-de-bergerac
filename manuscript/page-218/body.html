CHRISTIAN:
Were it not well
To change some words? Written haphazard-wise,
Will it fit Roxane?

CYRANO:
'Twill fit like a glove!

CHRISTIAN:
But. . .

CYRANO:
Ah, credulity of love! Roxane
Will think each word inspired by herself!

CHRISTIAN:
My friend!

(He throws himself into Cyrano's arms. They remain thus.)
