CHRISTIAN:
Show!

CYRANO:
Will you. . .?

CHRISTIAN (taking the letter):
Ay! (He opens and reads it):
Hold!

CYRANO:
What?

CHRISTIAN:
This little spot!

CYRANO (taking the letter, with an innocent look):
A spot?

CHRISTIAN:
A tear!
