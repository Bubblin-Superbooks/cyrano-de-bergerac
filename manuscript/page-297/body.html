ROXANE (quickly):
I have overlooked the postscript—see:—
'Give twenty pistoles for the Convent.'

THE FRIAR:
. . .Oh! Most worthy lord! (To Roxane):
Submit you?

ROXANE (with a martyr's look):
I submit! (While Ragueneau opens the door, and Christian invites the friar to enter, she whispers to Cyrano):
Oh, keep De Guiche at bay! He will be here! Let him not enter till. . .

CYRANO:
I understand! (To the friar):
What time need you to tie the marriage-knot?

THE FRIAR:
A quarter of an hour.
