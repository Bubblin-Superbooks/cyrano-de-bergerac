(Changing his tone as the porter goes away):
I have no money!. . .

LE BRET (with the action of throwing a bag):
How! The bag of crowns?. . .

CYRANO:
Paternal bounty, in a day, thou'rt sped!

LE BRET:
How live the next month?. . .

CYRANO:
I have nothing left.

LE BRET:
Folly!

CYRANO:
But what a graceful action! Think!
