THE FRIAR:
A letter.

CHRISTIAN:
What?

THE FRIAR (to Roxane):
Oh, it can boot but a holy business! 'Tis from a worthy lord. . .

ROXANE (to Christian):
De Guiche!

CHRISTIAN:
He dares. . .

ROXANE:
Oh, he will not importune me forever! (Unsealing the letter):
I love you,—therefore—
(She reads in a low voice by the aid of Ragueneau's
