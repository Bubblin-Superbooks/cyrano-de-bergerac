RAGUENEAU:
I ran—I saw. . .

LE BRET:
'Tis hideous!

RAGUENEAU:
Saw our poet, Sir—our friend—
Struck to the ground—a large wound in his head!

LE BRET:
He's dead?

RAGUENEAU:
No—but—I bore him to his room. . .
Ah! his room! What a thing to see!—that garret!

LE BRET:
He suffers?

RAGUENEAU:
No, his consciousness has flown.

LE BRET:
Saw you a doctor?
