ROXANE:
I love him; all is said. But you must know
I have only seen him at the Comedy. . .

CYRANO:
How? You have never spoken?

ROXANE:
Eyes can speak.

CYRANO:
How know you then that he. . .?

ROXANE:
Oh! people talk
'Neath the limes in the Place Royale. . .
Gossip's chat
Has let me know. . .
