CHRISTIAN (seizing his arm):
Oh, she is there!

(The window of the balcony is now lighted up.)

CYRANO (moved):
Her window!

CHRISTIAN:
Oh! I shall die!

CYRANO:
Speak lower!

CHRISTIAN (in a whisper):
I shall die!

CYRANO:
The night is dark. . .

CHRISTIAN:
Well!

CYRANO:
All can be repaired.
Although you merit not. Stand there, poor wretch!
