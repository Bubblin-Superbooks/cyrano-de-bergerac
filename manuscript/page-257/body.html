CHRISTIAN:
Oh utterly!

ROXANE:
Come, come!. . .unknot those tangled sentiments!

CHRISTIAN:
Your throat I'd kiss it!

ROXANE:
Christian!

CHRISTIAN:
I love thee!

ROXANE (half-rising):
Again!

CHRISTIAN (eagerly, detaining her):
No, no! I love thee not!

ROXANE (reseating herself):
'Tis well!

CHRISTIAN:
But I adore thee!
