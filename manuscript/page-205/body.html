CHRISTIAN:
My nose!. . .

(Every one starts up. Christian balances on his chair.)

CYRANO (in a choked voice):
. . .'My teeth! Who would break my teeth, and I, imprudent-like,
Was poking. . .'

CHRISTIAN:
My nose!. . .

CYRANO:
'My finger,. . .in the crack
Between the tree and bark! He may prove strong
And rap me. . .'

CHRISTIAN:
Over the nose. . .

CYRANO (wiping his forehead):
. . .'O' th' knuckles! Ay,'
But I cried, 'Forward, Gascon! Duty calls! On, Cyrano!'
