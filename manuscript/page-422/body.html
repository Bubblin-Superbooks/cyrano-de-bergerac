DE GUICHE (on the rampart—to the cadets—with fury):
Stand fast!

A VOICE (outside):
Lay down your arms!

THE CADETS:
No!

CYRANO (to De Guiche):
Now that you have proved your valor, Sir,
(Pointing to Roxane):
Fly, and save her!

DE GUICHE (rushing to Roxane, and carrying her away in his arms):
So be it! Gain but time,
The victory's ours!

CYRANO:
Good.
